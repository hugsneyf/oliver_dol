Transport

Bus

The popular transportation option in Rio de Janeiro, offering an extensive network that covers the city and its metropolitan area. With different lines, they provide an affordable and convenient way for passengers to travel to various parts of the city.

Taxi

By taxi, it is also refered to apps like Uber, 99, and Cabify as they are widely used by locals in Rio. These services offer convenience and speed, allowing passengers to easily move around the city, especially in hard-to-reach areas or during nighttime.

Train

The metropolitan train system is an important transportation option in Rio de Janeiro, connecting the city to neighboring municipalities. Trains are frequently used by commuters traveling to work or to explore areas beyond the city limits.

Bike

Rio de Janeiro has witnessed a growing use of bicycles as a means of transportation. With the implementation of bike lanes and the Bike Rio bike-sharing program, residents and tourists have the opportunity to enjoy the pleasant climate and beautiful scenery of the city while moving around in a sustainable way.
